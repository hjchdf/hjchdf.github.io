Vim总结(1): Text Objects

概述

text objects 字面应指代被选择的文本对象，此处为了方便表述，也指代使用这些文本对象时的选择模式。

  • 相较于多数编辑器以单个字符为单位的编辑方式，Vim可以将编辑对象扩展到单词、句子、段落(word, setence, paragraph)，提高编辑效率。这种更高级的上下文选择模式被称text objects。

    例如：ciw删除当前单词后进入插入模式

  • Vim对纯文本和多种编程语言都可以应用text objects，此外还可以通过Vim Script自定义新的text objects.

命令结构

  • number: optional，多次执行命令/对多个text objects对象执行操作
    • Vim固定用法，例如：2dd删除下面2行
  • command: 编辑操作，例如change, delete(cut), or yank(copy)
  • text object or motion: 指定操作范围
    • 可以是文本对象(text construct), e.g., word, sentence, paragraph，各种括号引号
    • 也可以是移动命令(motion), e.g., foward a line, back one page, end of the line

Text Construct范围

关键字

text construct 可以添加关键字进行更精准的范围选择：

  1. i：仅选择text construct，不包括空格
  2. a：选择text construct，包括后面的空格

word

  • Vim对word的定义(w)：

    A word consists of a sequence of letters, digits, and underscores, or a sequence of other non_blank characters, separated with white space (spaces, tabs, ).

    This can be changed with the iskeyword option.

    An empty line is also considered to be a word.

  • Vim对WORD的定义(W):

    A WORD consists of a sequence of non-blank characters, separated with white sapce.

    An empty line is also cnosidered to be a WORD.

  • 总结，word包含字母、数字、下划线和少数不常用字符，可以参考C的命名规则，WORD只要没有空格就行

  • 演示: 使用移动命令w，前进一个word; W,前进一个WORD

Sentence

  • Vim对sentence的定义(s)

    A sentence is defined as ending at a ., !, or ? followed by a line, or by a space, or tab. Any number or closing ), ], ", and ' characters may appear after the ., ! or ? before the space, tab or end of line.

    A paragraph and section boundary is also a setence boundary.

  • sentence以., !, ?结束，后面可以包含任意右括号，最后以空格、tab、行尾 分隔。

Paragraph

  • Vim对paragraph的定义(p):

    A paragraph begins after each empty line, and also at each of a set of paragraph macros, specified by the pairs of characters in the paragraphs` option.

Programming Language Text Objects

text objects 在编程语言中的常见用法

字符串

  • a" – a double quoted string(包含两端双引号，下同)

  • i" – inner double quoted string(不包含两端双引号，下同)

  • a' – a single quoted string

  • a' – inner single quoted string

  • a` – a back quoted string

  • i` – innner a back quoted stirng

    • vi"d – 只删除引号内部

    • va"d – 删除引号+内部+后方括号

括号

  • Parentheses
    • a) – a parenthesized block
    • i) – inner parentheized block
  • Brackets
    • a] – a bracketed block
    • i] – inner bracketed block
  • Braces
    • a} – a brace block
    • i} – inner brace block
